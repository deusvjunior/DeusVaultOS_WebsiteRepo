Brand Usage Guidelines

Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the symbol around all logo applications.

Minimum Size

Logo should never be smaller than 120px wide to maintain legibility and impact.

Color Variations

Use full-color version whenever possible. White/black versions only when color is not available.

Background Usage

Ensure sufficient contrast. Avoid placing logo on busy backgrounds or similar colors.
